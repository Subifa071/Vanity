Who We Are?

Cara is an exclusive importer, distributor, and retailer for major American and European cosmetics and skincare brands in Nepal. It remains the only Nepali business chain fully dedicated to beauty products and offers a wide range of products like makeup, skin care, masks, sunscreen, perfumes, makeup accessories among others. By now, Cara has authorization over more than twenty foreign beauty brands. We believe in inclusivity; therefore, our team is working on to recreate the beauty space into an open environment for everyone to explore.Cara has been working on a beauty campaign with a strong message of being and loving yourself, promoting our most cherished belief “True beauty is to be the real you”.

To use the power of beauty to bring the best in our customers-all the while inspiring and empowering women in every aspect of their lives.
